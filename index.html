Privacy Policy

Welcome to ExpenseTracker Pro. Your privacy is critically important to us. This policy outlines how we handle your enterprise and personal financial data.

1. Data Collection

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account, update your profile, or use our financial tracking tools. This includes authentication details, transactional records, and user preferences.

2. Data Usage

Your financial data is stored securely using Google Firebase infrastructure. It is used exclusively to provide you with the functionality of the application, including analytics, budget tracking, and account synchronization.

3. Security

We implement strict Firestore database rules to protect your personal information. Only authenticated users can access their specific tenant data. Biometric data is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.
